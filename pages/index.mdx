## <span className="text-purple-500 font-bold">Overview</span>
**Cloudinator** is a comprehensive deployment and management platform designed to streamline the process of application deployment for businesses and developers. It supports both monolithic and microservices architectures and offering customizable domain management.

## <span className="text-purple-500 font-bold">Objective</span>
This Guide will show you how to use this **Cloudinator** platform for deploy service both monolothic and spring microservice

- Getting familar with cloudinator
- Learn tutorial step by step of deployment
- How to create deployment service
